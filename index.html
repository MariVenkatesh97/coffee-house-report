Monthly Growth Trend

Sales have shown consistent month-over-month growth from January to June:

  • January: $58.8K
  • February: $76.1K
  • March: $81.7K
  • April: $118.5K
  • May: $156.7K
  • June: $165.5K

Key insight: Sales have nearly tripled over the six-month period, with the most significant acceleration observed between April-June.

Sales by Time of Day

Clear pattern of customer activity throughout the day:

  • Peak Period: 8-10 AM (highest at 9-10 AM)
  • Secondary Peak: 7-8 AM
  • Declining Pattern: Steady decline after 10 AM
  • Lowest Performance: Early morning (before 6 AM) and evening hours (after 7 PM)

Conclusion

The Coffee House business demonstrates robust health with consistent growth across all metrics. The strong upward sales trajectory from January to June indicates excellent market reception and effective business execution. Morning hours drive the majority of revenue, suggesting strong commuter and breakfast traffic.

Product performance is balanced across three equal-performing locations, with traditional coffee categories leading sales but specialty products showing promising growth. All products in the detailed view show positive growth trends, with premium and specialty items delivering the strongest percentage increases. The correlation between sales and volume growth confirms that higher prices are not driving revenue increases alone - customer demand is genuinely increasing across product lines.

The business appears well-positioned for continued expansion, with opportunities to further capitalize on morning peak hours while developing strategies to boost afternoon and evening sales.
